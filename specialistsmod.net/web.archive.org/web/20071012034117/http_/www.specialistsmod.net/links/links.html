PC Gamer (UK)
PC Gamer ran a brief article on TS in its December 2002 issue and also featured TS on the magazines monthly demo disc. Although this article is not on the web, screens of the TS portion of the magazine can be seen here.

3D Zone
Also in December of 2002, the enthusiastic interviewers of 3d Zone snagged an interview with John Matrix and Morfeo you can read the article (in english) at their site.

Holy Wars
This venerable Half-Life mod was a fast paced death match game that had players do battle in the afterlife to atone for sins. The TS team got help from the HW team for the TS bot code.

Multiplayer.it
The original Italian host for everything TS, Multiplayer.it provided web hosting for TS in the very begining.

Nupo.org
A modeling, skinning and mappig rescource a nice selection of models and other TS downloads and customizations can be found here.

E-Frag Quality Gaming
A UK based game server hosting company that has provided TS with its official server free of charge. See the news page for more information.

Wavelength
Wavelength.net is the current host of the TS webpage, and an outstanding place to go for Half-Life moding rescources. They say it best: " Wavelength is a website dedicated to all things game editing."

TGM Online
TGM Online also has an interview with TS creators Morfeo and John Matrix, this one is from October of 2003, and is in Italian.
